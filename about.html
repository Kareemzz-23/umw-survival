What we do ?

UMW survival guide is a resource hub for all medical students, regardless of year of study or level.
We aim to provide medical students with videos, presentations, notes, summaries and more. All resources are provided by other students who have passed their subjects, and excelled at university.
We also offer workshops and sessions that help people develop a grasp on basic concepts and gain a blueprint to follow for that subject. Our resources include academic as well as extracurricular information regarding university, associations, sports, and more. How can we help ?
